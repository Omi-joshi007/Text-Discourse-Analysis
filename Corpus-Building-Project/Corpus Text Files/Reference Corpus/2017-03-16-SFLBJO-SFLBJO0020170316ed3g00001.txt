Take a look inside the dine-in movie theater opening at CityPlace Doral (Photos) (Video)
CinéBistro, a 550-seat dine-in movie theater, opens Friday at the new CityPlace Doral and South Florida Business Journal photojournalist Jock Fistick got a look inside.
This CinéBistro will feature a big center bar with crafted cocktails and wines on the menu, and its restaurant will offer a "chef-driven American bistro menu" for both its movie and dine-in customers. A private dining room for parties and evens will also be available. Click on the slideshow for a first look at CinéBistro CityPlace Doral, opening March 17.
The theater created 110 jobs. It will be the 11th location developed by Cobb Theatres after it opened the flagship at the Dolphin Mall.
Along with the opening of the CinéBistro, a number of restaurants and retailers will be opening at CityPlace Doral on Friday, including Brimstone Woodfire Grill, Rusty Bucket Restaurant & Tavern, Cabo Flats Cantina & Tequila Bar, among other eateries and shops.
CityPlace Doral is being developed by  The Related Group[http://companies.bizjournals.com/profile/related/211602/?mkt=southflorida] and Shoma Group and will have 250,000 square feet of retail space; 40 dining, shopping and entertainment venues and 400 luxury residences. Its first phase opens this year.
